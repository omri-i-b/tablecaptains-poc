The flow, end to end

  1. The organizer sets up tiers in Table captains setup — Standard $2,500, Patron $5,000, Benefactor $10,000.
  2. A supporter clicks "Sponsor a table" and lands in the purchase flow — picks a tier, pays, done.
  3. They arrive at their captain portal via a link in their confirmation email — the seat-management page — where they fill 10 seats by sharing, inviting, or adding directly.
  4. The coordinator watches it all roll up in the guest list — grouped by captain, with sold-through, invited, and open seats on every row.

All four screens share state via localStorage under the key tablecaptains.data.v1. Editing a tier in the admin updates the purchase flow live; new packages added to the admin show up as selectable options on the captain-purchase page in the same session.
